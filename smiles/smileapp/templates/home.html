The London Smile Campaign

I wonder how many of you do what I do. I walk along the streets of London trying to make people smile. I do this by looking at people with a cheerful expression on my face, and giving them a gentle smile (not a grin; not a grimace). I reckon some 10% of passers-by smile back. This gives me a great feeling of pleasant satisfaction; it also gives them, I am assured, a feeling of happiness.

Your first thoughts might be "This is childish and ridiculous". But is it? If we could get 20% or more of passers-by exchanging this type of friendly greeting, we could alter the entire emotional atmosphere of London, for the better. Why don't you give it a go - join the London Smile Campaign, and make yourself and others happier. Ian Niall Rankin
